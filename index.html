≡ Key Definitions

Visibility Score (0–100)
A proprietary composite score measuring how prominently a grocer appears across AI platform responses. Factors include mention frequency, position rank (listed 1st vs. 5th), mention type (primary recommendation vs. comparison), and sentiment. Higher is better.
Mention Types
Primary Recommendation — the AI directly recommends the grocer. Alternative Option — mentioned as another choice. Comparison Mention — named alongside competitors. Negative Mention — referenced unfavorably. Neutral Mention — named without clear sentiment.
Target Market
A geographic region where queries are tailored with local context (e.g., "best grocery in Des Moines" vs. "best grocery in St. Louis"). Allows per-market visibility benchmarking.
Pipeline Run
A single automated execution cycle that queries all 5 AI platforms for all 40 queries, parses the responses, detects grocer mentions, and computes updated visibility scores. Runs daily at 6:00 AM CT.
Agentic Commerce
The emerging paradigm where AI systems act as purchasing intermediaries — recommending products, stores, and brands on behalf of consumers. Visibility in this channel is the next frontier of retail marketing.
Recommendation Engine
An automated system that analyzes visibility data across platforms and generates strategic recommendations — identifying blind spots, upgrade opportunities, competitive strengths, and reputation risks.

◇ Product Vision

Claravoy is designed to evolve from a monitoring dashboard into a full agentic commerce optimization platform.

Phase 1 — Now
Visibility monitoring, daily automated scoring, competitive benchmarking, and AI-generated recommendations for grocery chains.
Phase 2 — Growth
Multi-grocer SaaS platform. Self-service onboarding, white-label dashboards, expanded AI platform coverage, and historical trend analytics.
Phase 3 — Platform
Bidirectional intelligence — selling anonymized visibility insights back to CPG brands and food manufacturers. "What are AI platforms recommending to consumers?" becomes a data product.
Phase 4 — Optimize
Active optimization tools that help grocers influence their AI visibility — content recommendations, structured data strategies, and AI-specific brand positioning.
